2.50 GCD

Choosing this 2.50 set is highly recommended. It is much easier to play, more comfortable and will thus likely yield better results in practice.

Compatibility to Reaper sets is very limited.

2.46 GCD

In case you prefer a slightly faster set. Can be preferable in some fights due to better GCD alignment but is much harder to play optimally. Requires you to get nine GCD buff windows for equal performance to the 2.50 set.

TOP BiS 2.46 (10,542 dps)

Syncing gear is currently not a viable option for this fight due to the massive loss of substats from materia. The relic weapon is an exception.

TOP BiS 2.50 (10,504 dps)

DSR BiS

Syncing gear is currently not a viable option for this fight due to the massive loss of substats from materia. The relic weapon is an exception.

TEA BiS

Gear pieces with equal stats can be used instead of the listed i600 pieces as long as they are above i595.

UWU BiS

!!Example set!! You have many options for each gear slot. Synced gear has to be at least i500. Please refer to etro or ariyala for other options. Materia does not matter in synced gear.

UCoB BiS

!!Example set!! You have many options for each gear slot. Synced gear has to be at least i470. Please refer to etro or ariyala for other options. Materia does not matter in synced gear.
